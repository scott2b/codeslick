wordZearch

One of several projects in the area of narrative content generation. WordZearch.com was a fun project that I did to showcase another type of content generation – automated puzzle generation.

The wordZearch administrative panel provides a means for auto-generating a wordsearch puzzle from a list of words. Additionally, the wordZearch backend automatically generates a daily wordsearch puzzle from terms gleaned from the day’s news topics.
