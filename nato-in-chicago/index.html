Nato in Chicago

Project to showcase concepts of innovation and technology in journalism around the event of the NATO summit in Chicago in May, 2012.

Part of the ongoing initiative at the Knight Lab, I contributed social media aggregation, and deployment and site maintenance to this project, which was rapidly produced in the weeks before the event by a team of Knight Lab and Northwestern faculty, staff, and students.
